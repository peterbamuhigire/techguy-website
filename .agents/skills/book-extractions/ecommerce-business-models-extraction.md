# Sanders - Ecommerce Business Models

**Source:** Mark C. Sanders, *E-Commerce Business Models*.

## Use this for

- Traffic and retargeting basics
- Fulfilment-aware ecommerce strategy
- Marketplace versus owned-channel decisions

## Keep only the durable parts

This book is heavily Amazon FBA oriented and includes hype-driven claims. Do not import its promises or marketplace-specific advice blindly. Keep only the structural lessons.

## Durable insights

1. The business model changes the website job.
2. Traffic control matters after the first click; retargeting and remarketing recover value that generic storefront design misses.
3. Fulfilment, shipping, and operational rules shape trust as much as page design does.
4. Marketplace-led sellers need an owned audience strategy or they remain dependent on rented channels.
