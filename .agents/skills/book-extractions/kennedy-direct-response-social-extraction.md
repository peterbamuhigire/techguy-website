# Kennedy / Walsh-Phillips - Direct Response Social

**Source:** *No B.S. Guide to Direct Response Social Media Marketing*.

## Use this for

- Social media tied to measurable business outcomes
- Offer-led campaigns
- Pixel, list, retargeting, and follow-up logic

## High-signal insights

1. Social media is not the business goal; response is.
2. Every campaign needs a tracked CTA, dedicated destination, and follow-up path.
3. Likes and reach are weak proxies. Measure leads, appointments, sales, and cost per outcome.
4. Social performs best when paired with a clear offer, landing page, and retargeting.
5. Social proof and repeated visibility reduce hesitation, but only when the promise is specific.
