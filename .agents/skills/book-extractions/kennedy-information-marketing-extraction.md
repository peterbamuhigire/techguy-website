# Kennedy / Glazer / Skrob - Information Marketing

**Source:** *The Official Get Rich Guide to Information Marketing*.

## Use this for

- Lead-magnet design
- Landing-page architecture
- Authority offers
- Education-led conversion paths

## High-signal insights

1. Use the power of one: one audience, one problem, one promise, one page goal.
2. Landing pages outperform general homepages when the campaign has a defined offer.
3. Education can be a commercial asset if every lesson points toward a next step.
4. Opt-in flow quality depends on salutation, presentation, consummation, perseverance, and enticement: attract, present, close, follow up, and continue the relationship.
5. List building is an owned asset; reach without capture has weak long-term value.

## Filter out

- Overheated hype language
- US-only assumptions
- Scarcity theatre that the business cannot substantiate
