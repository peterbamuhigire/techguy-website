## Synthesis

The existing skills already cover many specialist pieces: general content clarity, sales copy, long-form direct response, blog writing, page building, SEO/GEO, storytelling, and buyer-question content strategy. The gap was not another narrow copywriting framework. The gap was a shared premium writing layer that forces all commercial writing to connect strategy, buyer language, proof, offer clarity, SEO/AI-search readiness, and editorial polish.

The strongest durable patterns across the reviewed material are:

- Start with market, message, offer, and page job before drafting.
- Use buyer language, objections, sales notes, reviews, and proof as the raw material for serious copy.
- Make value propositions clear, specific, desirable, and testable before making them stylish.
- Treat CTAs as commitment design: they should clarify the reward, next step, and anxiety reducers.
- Place proof near the claim or action that creates doubt.
- Use educational content as authority, trust-building, sales enablement, and search demand capture.
- Build content systems with clusters, series, and follow-up paths instead of isolated posts.
- Use direct-response structure selectively and ethically, with sector restraint and real urgency only.
- Make content answerable for AI/search systems without weakening human trust or usefulness.

## Standards Added

The new `premium-commercial-writing` skill adds:

- a cross-cutting workflow for premium website, SEO, blog, landing page, offer, and document writing
- professional writing QA focused on proof, claims, tone, structure, and implementation readiness
- page-type writing patterns for home, service, about, pricing, contact, case study, and FAQ pages
- offer and landing-page writing guidance for campaigns, consultations, lead magnets, high-ticket services, and productized services
- blog and authority writing guidance for article types, clusters, author signals, and sales enablement
- SEO and AI-search writing guidance for intent, entity clarity, answer blocks, schema handoff, and crawler-file readiness
- sales-enablement and document-writing guidance for proposals, briefs, lead magnets, one-pagers, and case-study documents

## Rejected Or Downgraded Patterns

- fake scarcity
- pressure tactics that damage premium trust
- generic growth language
- slogan-led heroes
- unsupported authority claims
- keyword stuffing
- AI-answer optimization that produces thin content
- copying or closely paraphrasing source frameworks into skill files

## Routing Decision

Do not replace the existing writing skills. Use `premium-commercial-writing` as the shared quality layer:

- `content-writing` for general copy clarity and reader empathy
- `sales-copywriting` for conversion messaging and CTA work
- `long-form-sales-copy` for full sales pages and VSL-style structures
- `blog-writer` for article production and publishing
- `brand-storytelling` for narrative and identity-driven pages
- `they-ask-you-answer` for buyer-question strategy and radical transparency
- `seo` for implementation of metadata, schema, crawler files, and AI visibility
- `page-builder` for implementation-facing page production

The new layer should be loaded when a deliverable must be premium-fee worthy across several of those concerns at once.
