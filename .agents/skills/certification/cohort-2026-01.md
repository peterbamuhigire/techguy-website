## Certificate ID scheme

`ENG-<track>-<cohort>-<seq>` where:
- `track` is `T1` (Strategist), `T2` (Designer/Builder), `T3` (SEO+Content),
  or `T4` (QA+Launch).
- `cohort` is `2026-01` for this cohort.
- `seq` is the two-digit sequence within this track for this cohort.

Example: `ENG-T2-2026-01-03` is the third designer/builder to pass in the
January 2026 cohort.

## Certificate issuance

On pass, the programme lead:

1. Fills the operator row with `ENG-<track>-2026-01-<seq>` and issue date.
2. Generates a certificate PDF signed by the programme lead (template in
   `certification/templates/certificate.md` — see future Phase 12 work).
3. Adds the operator (with written consent) to the public certified list at
   `dashboards/public-scorecard.md`, section "Certified operators".
4. Sends onboarding to the certified-operator network channel.
