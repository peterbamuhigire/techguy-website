# Founder Story Templates

## The Founding Story Framework

Use this to write the agency About page, LinkedIn summary, and speaker bio.

### Template Structure (fill in the brackets):

---

**The problem I saw:**
When I started [Agency Name], I kept seeing the same thing happen to good businesses in [region/sector]: [describe the specific pattern — e.g., "they were paying for beautiful websites that nobody could find, and losing clients to competitors with uglier sites but better Google rankings"].

**Why I cared:**
This wasn't just a business problem. [Personal connection — e.g., "I watched my uncle's tailoring business lose customers to a competitor who'd invested in digital marketing, even though my uncle's work was objectively better. The better product was losing because of poor visibility."]

**What I tried first:**
My early attempts weren't perfect. [Honest failure — e.g., "I built sites that looked great but didn't convert. I thought good design was enough. It wasn't."] That gap between "looks good" and "works commercially" became the problem I dedicated my practice to solving.

**The breakthrough:**
Everything changed when I realised [insight — e.g., "a website is not a brochure. It's a sales conversation happening 24 hours a day, and it either handles that conversation well or it doesn't. Once I started designing with that question — 'what does this visitor need to believe before they pick up the phone?' — results changed completely."]

**What clients get because of this:**
Today, [Agency Name] is built around one principle: [core belief — e.g., "every design decision must serve the business goal, not the designer's portfolio"]. Our clients in [region] get websites that don't just win awards — they win clients.

**The invitation:**
If your current website isn't generating the leads and credibility your business deserves, [specific next step — e.g., "let's spend 20 minutes looking at what's costing you most. No pitch. Just a diagnosis."]

---

## The 60-Second Verbal Positioning Statement

Use this when asked "so what do you do?" at networking events:

"I run [Agency Name] — we build websites and digital marketing systems for [target sector] businesses that want to attract premium clients. Most of our clients come to us because their current website looks fine but isn't generating leads. We diagnose what's blocking conversions, redesign around the client journey, and then optimise monthly until the site is genuinely doing sales work for the business. Our clients typically see [specific result] within [timeframe]."

## The Case Study Hero's Journey Template

### Title Formula: "[Client Name] Increased [Metric] by [%] in [Timeframe]"

**Act 1: The Client Before**
[Client Name] is a [business type] in [location]. When they came to us, they were [describe situation in their words if possible — use empathy, not judgment]. Their website was [specific problem — e.g., "ranking on page 4 for their main service term, generating fewer than 2 enquiries per month"]. Despite [strength they had — e.g., "excellent reviews and strong word-of-mouth"], their online presence wasn't reflecting the quality of their work.

**The Conflict**
They had tried [previous attempt]. It hadn't worked because [real reason — be specific]. The cost was [impact of the problem — in business terms, not just emotion].

**Act 2: What We Did Together**
We started with a [diagnostic/audit]. What we found: [2-3 specific findings]. Based on this, we [approach taken — in plain language, not jargon]. The process took [timeframe].

**Act 3: The Result**
Within [timeframe], [Client Name] saw [specific, measurable result]. [Direct quote from client if possible.] Today, [current state — "the website is their top source of new business, generating an average of X enquiries per month."]

**The Transformation Line (client's own words or paraphrase):**
"[Before/after contrast in one sentence.]"

## Authority Bio (for speaking and partnerships)

**Short version (50 words):**
[Founder Name] is the founder of [Agency Name], a [location]-based agency specialising in [niche]. [He/She/They] has helped [X] businesses across [region] transform their digital presence into their top source of qualified leads. [Agency Name] is [notable credential or result].

**Long version (150 words):**
[Founder Name] founded [Agency Name] after [brief origin]. Since then, [he/she/they] has worked with [type of clients] across [region] to [outcome]. [Agency Name]'s approach — combining diagnostic strategy with conversion-focused design and ongoing optimisation — has helped clients achieve [result range].

[Founder Name] speaks regularly on [topics] to [audiences], and contributes to [publications/forums] on [subject]. [He/She/They] believes [core positioning belief] — and has built [Agency Name] to prove it.

Clients include [2-3 notable names or sectors if available]. Based in [location], [Agency Name] serves clients across [region].
