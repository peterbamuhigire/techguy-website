# Information Marketing Assets

Use this reference when the agency needs authority assets that generate leads, qualify
buyers, or create new revenue without diluting the premium positioning.

## Principle

Package expertise in a way that increases trust and shortens the sales cycle. The asset
must demonstrate judgment, not just broadcast content volume.

## Recommended asset types

| Asset | Best Use | Constraint |
|---|---|---|
| Paid audit | Monetize diagnosis and surface high-fit prospects | Must have a clear rubric and output |
| Workshop | Educate decision makers and create urgency | Needs one outcome and one next step |
| Newsletter | Build owned audience and proof-of-thinking | Must stay insight-led, not generic tips |
| Scorecard / benchmark | Turn standards into a lead magnet | Needs a strong interpretation layer |
| Playbook | Productize a proven method | Must point toward implementation support |

## Guardrails

- Do not create broad "digital marketing tips" assets. Stay inside the agency category.
- Do not make the free asset so vague that only the sales call explains the value.
- Do not let the productized asset undercut the premium engagement.
- Every asset should end with a clear ascension path: audit, workshop, strategy call, or project application.
