# Post-Launch Review Checklist

Use this for launch-day, 7-day, and 30-day reviews.
The goal is to confirm that the site stayed stable and that the business-critical flows are intact.

## Launch-Day Review

- [ ] Site loads on primary domain
- [ ] SSL is valid
- [ ] Primary navigation works
- [ ] Primary CTA works
- [ ] Core forms submit and deliver
- [ ] Analytics is collecting data
- [ ] robots.txt and sitemap are accessible
- [ ] No critical console errors on priority pages

## 7-Day Review

- [ ] Uptime checks show no significant outage
- [ ] Form delivery remains stable
- [ ] No broken links reported
- [ ] Search Console or indexing signals look normal
- [ ] Analytics baseline is recorded
- [ ] Client has reported no unresolved critical issue

## 30-Day Review

- [ ] Performance trend reviewed
- [ ] Conversion events reviewed
- [ ] Top pages and search entry points reviewed
- [ ] Content or proof gaps observed after launch are recorded
- [ ] CRO or SEO follow-up opportunities identified
- [ ] Maintenance or retainer recommendation prepared where relevant
