# App Landing Page Branding & Visual Identity

App landing pages have one job: convert visitors into downloads or signups. Every design decision must serve conversion. The page must communicate: "This app solves YOUR problem, it's trustworthy, and getting started is effortless."

## Color Psychology for App Landing Pages

| Hue | Psychological Effect | Best For | Avoid When |
|-----|---------------------|----------|------------|
| **Electric Blue** (#3B82F6) | Trust, professionalism, reliability | Productivity, enterprise, finance | Creative/entertainment apps |
| **Emerald** (#10B981) | Growth, health, success, "go" | Health, finance, sustainability apps | Gaming, entertainment |
| **Purple** (#7C3AED) | Innovation, creativity, premium | AI/ML, creative tools, social apps | Healthcare, finance |
| **Coral** (#F97316) | Energy, urgency, action | Fitness, food, marketplace apps | Finance, medical, enterprise |
| **Rose** (#E11D48) | Passion, connection, excitement | Dating, social, lifestyle apps | Enterprise, B2B tools |
| **Cyan** (#06B6D4) | Fresh, modern, clear | Communication, productivity, dev tools | Luxury, traditional markets |
| **Amber** (#F59E0B) | Optimism, warmth, attention | Education, kids, casual gaming | Enterprise, medical |
| **Slate** (#475569) | Sophisticated, neutral, premium | Developer tools, enterprise SaaS | Consumer apps, kids |

### CTA Button Color Strategy

The CTA button must have the **highest contrast on the page**:

| Page Primary | CTA Color | Why |
|-------------|-----------|-----|
| Blue theme | Orange/coral CTA | Complementary contrast |
| Green theme | Purple or coral CTA | Cross-spectrum contrast |
| Dark theme | Bright accent CTA | Light on dark contrast |
| Light theme | Bold saturated CTA | Demands attention |
| Purple theme | Green or amber CTA | Warm vs cool contrast |

**Rule:** CTA colour should appear NOWHERE else on the page except buttons.

### Competitive Differentiation

| App Category | Overused Colors | Differentiation Territory |
|--------------|----------------|--------------------------|
| **Productivity** | Blue + white | Warm neutrals, sage green, coral accents |
| **Finance** | Navy + green | Rich purple, warm gold, charcoal |
| **Health** | Green + white | Terracotta, warm blue, sunset tones |
| **Social** | Gradient purple-pink | Monochrome + one bold accent |
| **Gaming** | Neon + black | Retro palettes, pastels, unexpected |
| **Education** | Blue + yellow | Rich burgundy, forest green, sophisticated |

## Typography for App Landing Pages

| App Type | Header Font | Body Font | Personality |
|---------|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| **SaaS/Productivity** | Bold geometric sans (Outfit, Space Grotesk) | Clean sans (DM Sans) | Modern, efficient |
| **Consumer/Lifestyle** | Friendly rounded sans (Nunito, Quicksand) | Readable sans (Source Sans Pro) | Approachable, warm |
| **Developer Tools** | Monospace display (JetBrains Mono) | Technical sans (Inter) | Technical, precise |
| **Creative Tools** | Display font (Clash Display, Cabinet Grotesk) | Clean sans (Satoshi) | Bold, expressive |
| **Enterprise** | Professional serif or sans (DM Serif, Plus Jakarta Sans) | Clean sans (DM Sans) | Trustworthy, established |
| **Kids/Education** | Rounded, playful (Baloo 2, Fredoka) | Readable sans (Nunito Sans) | Fun, accessible |

### Headline Copy Principles

1. **Benefit first, feature second**: "Save 5 hours a week" not "AI-powered scheduler"
2. **One clear value proposition**: Don't list features in the hero
3. **Specific over vague**: "Used by 50,000 teams" not "Trusted by many"
4. **Action-oriented**: "Start free" not "Learn more"

## Screenshot & Visual Strategy

### App Screenshot Rules

| Guideline | Rationale |
|-----------|-----------|
| **Show real UI** | Mockups feel dishonest; real screenshots build trust |
| **3-5 screens maximum** | More overwhelms; less feels incomplete |
| **Highlight key feature** | First screenshot = primary value proposition |
| **Device frame optional** | Clean mockup frames work; raw screenshots also work |
| **Annotate sparingly** | Brief callouts OK; don't overlay text on every screen |

### Hero Visual Options

| Approach | Best For | Conversion Impact |
|----------|----------|-------------------|
| **App screenshot in device frame** | Most apps | High — shows exactly what they get |
| **Short demo video (15-30s)** | Complex apps | Very high — 73% increase |
| **Animated UI walkthrough** | Interactive tools | High — shows flow |
| **Illustration + screenshot** | Lifestyle/consumer apps | Medium — sets mood |

### Video on Landing Pages

- **YouTube-hosted only** (per project standards)
- Embed via `youtube-nocookie.com` with `loading="lazy"`
- Auto-play muted with captions visible
- Maximum 60 seconds for landing page videos
- Full demo videos on separate page

## Brand Voice for App Landing Pages

| Section | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Hero headline** | Bold, benefit-focused | "Your meetings, finally organised" |
| **Subheadline** | Specific, credible | "Join 50,000 teams saving 5 hours/week" |
| **Feature cards** | Clear, concise | "One-click scheduling. No back-and-forth." |
| **Social proof** | Authentic, specific | "Cut our meeting prep time by 80%" — Sarah, PM at Stripe |
| **CTA** | Action-oriented, low-friction | "Start free — no credit card" |
| **FAQ** | Helpful, honest | Address real objections directly |

### Copy Frameworks

| Framework | Structure | Best For |
|-----------|-----------|----------|
| **PAS** | Problem → Agitate → Solve | Pain-point heavy apps |
| **AIDA** | Attention → Interest → Desire → Action | Consumer apps |
| **FAB** | Feature → Advantage → Benefit | Feature-rich tools |
| **BAB** | Before → After → Bridge | Transformation apps |
