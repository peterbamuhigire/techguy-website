# Client Conversion & Trust Design

B2B websites convert at 2-5% on average. Top-performing consultancy sites achieve 8-12% by designing for the decision-maker's journey: awareness → credibility → engagement → contact. The sales cycle is long (weeks to months), so the website must build trust at every touchpoint.

## The 6-Second Test

A C-suite visitor evaluates your site in 6 seconds. The hero must:

- **Lead with value, not identity**: "We help enterprises reduce costs by 30%" beats "Welcome to Acme Consulting"
- **Show client logos**: Trust bar with 4-6 recognisable logos immediately below hero
- **One primary CTA**: "Schedule a Consultation", "Download Our Report", or "View Case Studies" — not all three
- **Credential visible**: "Trusted by 200+ enterprises since 2008" or industry certification badge

## The B2B Conversion Funnel

```
[Awareness]       →  [Credibility]     →  [Engagement]      →  [Conversion]
Homepage hero        Case studies         Thought leadership    Contact/demo
LinkedIn/search      Client logos         Webinars/reports      Schedule call
Referral             Team credentials     Blog/insights         RFP response
```

### Page Flow
1. **Homepage**: Hero + value prop → Trust bar (logos) → Services grid → Case study highlights → Stats → CTA
2. **Services**: Service cards → Methodology → Results/outcomes → Related case studies → Enquiry
3. **Case Studies**: Challenge → Approach → Results (with metrics) → Client quote → Related services
4. **About/Team**: Company story → Leadership bios → Values → Awards/credentials → Careers
5. **Insights**: Blog/whitepaper grid → Featured research → Newsletter signup → Author bios
6. **Contact**: Enquiry form → Office locations → Phone/email → Map

## Trust Architecture

### Tier 1: Must-Have (Every Page)
- **Client logos**: 4-6 recognisable logos in a trust bar
- **Years in business**: "Established 2005" signals stability
- **Industry certifications**: ISO, professional body memberships
- **Contact information**: Phone + email visible in header/footer

### Tier 2: High-Impact (Homepage + Services)
- **Case study metrics**: "$2.4M saved", "30% efficiency gain", "6-month ROI"
- **Team credentials**: Advanced degrees, certifications, years experience
- **Client testimonials**: Named, titled, with company logo
- **Awards**: "Top 10 Consulting Firm 2025", industry recognition
- **Media mentions**: "As featured in..." publication logos

### Tier 3: Depth (About + Insights)
- **Thought leadership**: Published research, conference speaking, whitepapers
- **Methodology documentation**: Proprietary frameworks with visual diagrams
- **Financial transparency**: Revenue growth, team size, office locations
- **CSR/ESG**: Community involvement, sustainability commitments
- **Partner ecosystem**: Technology partners, alliance memberships

## Case Study Design

Case studies are the #1 conversion tool for B2B. Design pattern:

```
[Logo/Industry badge]
Challenge: What the client faced (2-3 sentences)
Approach: What you did (bullet points)
Results: Quantified outcomes (3-4 key metrics, large font)
"Quote from client CTO/CEO" — Name, Title, Company
[View Full Case Study →]
```

### Case Study Card
- Industry badge/icon at top
- Client name or anonymised ("Global Logistics Company")
- 1-sentence challenge summary
- 3 key metrics in bold (revenue, time saved, ROI)
- Read more CTA

## CTA Hierarchy

| CTA | Style | Placement | Priority |
|-----|-------|-----------|----------|
| **Schedule Consultation** | Primary, bold | Hero, service pages, page bottom | Highest |
| **Download Report/Whitepaper** | Secondary, gated | Insights, sidebar, exit-intent | High |
| **View Case Studies** | Text link or card | Homepage, service pages | Medium |
| **Subscribe to Insights** | Tertiary, newsletter | Blog, footer | Lower |
| **Contact Us** | Standard | Header, footer | Baseline |

## Thought Leadership Design

Insights/blog section is critical for SEO and authority:

- **Featured article**: Large hero image + title + excerpt
- **Category filters**: By industry, service line, or topic
- **Author attribution**: Photo, name, title, bio link
- **Download gates**: Email capture for whitepapers/reports
- **Related content**: "You may also be interested in..." sidebar
- **Social sharing**: LinkedIn prominent (primary B2B channel)

## Common Mistakes
1. **"Welcome to our website"** hero text — wastes the 6-second window
2. **No client logos** — the fastest trust signal, missing on most B2B sites
3. **Generic stock photos** — "business people shaking hands" kills credibility
4. **Buried case studies** — should be 1-2 clicks from homepage
5. **No metrics in case studies** — "We helped a client" vs "$2.4M revenue increase"
6. **Too many CTAs** — one primary action per page
7. **No thought leadership** — signals the firm has nothing to say
8. **Missing team photos** — people buy from people, not logos
