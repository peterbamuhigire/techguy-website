# Corporate & Consulting Branding & Visual Identity

Corporate websites sell credibility before services. Decision-makers (C-suite, procurement) spend an average of 6 seconds evaluating whether a consultancy "looks" capable. Visual authority — colors, typography, white space — is the first filter before any content is read.

## Color Psychology for Corporate

| Hue | Psychological Effect | Best For | Avoid When |
|-----|---------------------|----------|------------|
| **Deep Navy** (#1E3A5F) | Trust, authority, stability | Management consulting, finance, law | You want to feel innovative/disruptive |
| **Slate Blue** (#475569) | Professional, calm, reliable | IT consulting, enterprise services | Creative agencies, startups |
| **Forest Green** (#166534) | Growth, sustainability, wealth | ESG consulting, investment, agriculture | Fast-paced tech consulting |
| **Charcoal** (#1C1917) | Sophistication, premium, power | Strategy firms, private equity | Community-focused organisations |
| **Deep Teal** (#0F766E) | Innovation within trust, modern authority | Digital transformation, tech advisory | Traditional/heritage firms |
| **Burgundy** (#7F1D1D) | Prestige, heritage, gravitas | Established firms, luxury advisory | Startup/agile consultancies |
| **Steel Gray** (#374151) | Neutrality, balance, expertise | Audit firms, compliance, legal advisory | Marketing/creative consultancies |
| **Royal Purple** (#581C87) | Wisdom, innovation, strategy | Management strategy, brand consulting | Conservative financial services |

### Competitive Differentiation

- If every consultancy in your market uses navy + white, choose slate + gold
- If Big Four competitors are blue, differentiate with teal or forest green
- Boutique firms: bolder accent colors signal specialisation vs generic corporate

**Real examples:** McKinsey uses deep blue for authority. Bain uses red for energy. BCG uses green for growth. Deloitte uses green to differentiate from blue competitors. Your firm needs its own territory.

## Typography for Corporate

| Firm Type | Header Font | Body Font | Personality |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Strategy/Management** | Elegant serif (DM Serif Display, Cormorant) | Clean sans (DM Sans, Inter) | Authoritative, premium |
| **Technology/Digital** | Modern geometric (Outfit, Space Grotesk) | Humanist sans (Source Sans Pro) | Innovative, precise |
| **Financial/Investment** | Classic serif (EB Garamond, Lora) | Neutral sans (Inter, Nunito Sans) | Established, trustworthy |
| **Creative Consulting** | Display sans (Clash Display, Satoshi) | Clean sans (DM Sans) | Bold, distinctive |
| **Legal/Compliance** | Traditional serif (Cormorant, Literata) | Readable sans (Source Sans Pro) | Formal, credible |
| **Boutique/Specialist** | Distinctive serif or sans (Fraunces, General Sans) | Warm sans (Plus Jakarta Sans) | Personal, expert |

### Key Rules
- Body text: 16px minimum, 1.6 line-height (decision-makers scan quickly)
- White space: generous — premium brands breathe
- Never use: Comic Sans, Papyrus, or overly playful fonts in corporate contexts

## Photography Direction

- **Team photos**: Professional headshots with consistent background/lighting
- **Office/workspace**: Modern, clean, collaborative spaces
- **Client interactions**: Meeting rooms, workshops, presentations (authentic, not staged)
- **Data/results**: Charts, dashboards, reports (showing competence)
- **Events**: Conferences, speaking engagements, industry panels
- **Never use stock**: Generic "handshake" or "diverse team in boardroom" stock photos destroy credibility instantly

## Brand Voice

| Audience | Tone | Example |
|----------|------|---------|
| **C-suite prospects** | Confident, strategic, concise | "Strategy that delivers measurable results" |
| **Procurement/RFP** | Professional, precise, evidence-based | "15 years, 200+ engagements, Fortune 500 clients" |
| **Talent/recruits** | Inspiring, growth-focused | "Join a team that shapes industry futures" |
| **Media/analysts** | Thought leadership, authority | "Our latest research on market transformation" |
