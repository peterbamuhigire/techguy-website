# Corporate Type Design Patterns

No two consultancies should look the same. Each corporate type has distinct trust signals, buyer expectations, and positioning.

## Strategy & Management Consulting

**Audience:** C-suite, board members, private equity, government
**Trust signals:** Case study outcomes, team credentials, methodology, thought leadership

- **Palette:** Deep navy + gold accent. Premium, authoritative.
- **Typography:** Elegant serif headers + clean sans body. Generous letter-spacing.
- **Photography:** Executive team, boardroom settings, strategic frameworks visualised.
- **Tone:** Confident, strategic, concise. "Shaping enterprises for what's next."
- **Key components:** Methodology diagram, case study gallery or featured case study stack, leadership grid, insights hub, partnership logos
- **Unique:** Proprietary framework visualisation, industry expertise matrix, client impact timeline

## Technology & Digital Consulting

**Audience:** CTOs, IT directors, digital transformation leads
**Trust signals:** Technical certifications, technology partnerships, delivery track record

- **Palette:** Deep teal + electric accent. Modern, innovative.
- **Typography:** Geometric sans (Outfit, Space Grotesk). Tech-forward.
- **Photography:** Modern offices, code/dashboards (anonymised), team collaboration.
- **Tone:** Expert, forward-thinking. "Engineering digital futures."
- **Key components:** Technology stack showcase, architecture diagrams, sprint methodology, cert badges, demo booking
- **Unique:** Interactive tech stack selector, ROI calculator, integration partner grid

## Financial & Investment Advisory

**Audience:** CFOs, investors, fund managers, high-net-worth individuals
**Trust signals:** Regulatory compliance, AUM figures, fund performance, credentials

- **Palette:** Charcoal + gold or forest green. Conservative, established.
- **Typography:** Classic serif (EB Garamond) + neutral sans. Formal precision.
- **Photography:** Minimal — data visualisations, skylines, abstract patterns preferred.
- **Tone:** Measured, precise, trustworthy. "Prudent strategies for lasting wealth."
- **Key components:** Performance charts, compliance badges, team credentials, fund comparison tables, disclaimer notices
- **Unique:** Fund performance visualiser, risk calculator, regulatory compliance section

## Creative & Brand Consulting

**Audience:** CMOs, brand managers, marketing directors, founders
**Trust signals:** Portfolio quality, awards, client brand transformations, creative recognition

- **Palette:** Bold, distinctive. Black + vibrant accent, or unexpected combinations. Never generic blue.
- **Typography:** Display fonts (Clash Display, Satoshi). Strong visual personality.
- **Photography:** Creative work — brand identity projects, campaigns, visual systems.
- **Tone:** Bold, distinctive, creative. "Brands that move people."
- **Key components:** Portfolio showcase (large images), before/after brand transformations, awards wall, creative process timeline
- **Unique:** Interactive portfolio with project filtering, brand identity case studies, creative brief generator

## Legal & Compliance Consulting

**Audience:** General counsel, compliance officers, risk managers
**Trust signals:** Bar admissions, jurisdictions, precedent cases, regulatory expertise

- **Palette:** Navy or burgundy + slate. Conservative, authoritative.
- **Typography:** Traditional serif headers (Cormorant) + readable sans body. Formal.
- **Photography:** Office interiors, courtroom/regulatory settings, team portraits.
- **Tone:** Authoritative, precise, reassuring. "Legal certainty in uncertain times."
- **Key components:** Practice area grid, attorney profiles (detailed credentials), publication list, jurisdiction map, consultation booking
- **Unique:** Jurisdiction expertise map, regulatory update feed, attorney experience comparison

## Boutique & Specialist Firms

**Audience:** Niche buyers seeking deep expertise in a specific domain
**Trust signals:** Domain depth, personal reputation, referral network, thought leadership

- **Palette:** Personal, distinctive. Departs from corporate blue — uses warm or unexpected colours.
- **Typography:** Distinctive pairing that reflects founder personality.
- **Photography:** Founder-centric, personal branding, niche expertise visualised.
- **Tone:** Personal, expert, opinionated. "Deep expertise, not a wide net."
- **Key components:** Founder story, niche expertise showcase, client relationship testimonials, publication/speaking list
- **Unique:** Founder bio as hero, niche expertise depth chart, personal brand photography

## Branding Personality Framework

| Trait | Spectrum | Design Impact |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| **Scale** | Boutique ←→ Enterprise | White space, team size messaging, client logos |
| **Innovation** | Traditional ←→ Disruptive | Typography boldness, colour saturation, layout |
| **Authority** | Collaborative ←→ Authoritative | Voice tone, imagery, credential prominence |
| **Formality** | Approachable ←→ Formal | Serif vs sans, spacing, photography style |
| **Specialisation** | Generalist ←→ Niche | Service breadth display, expertise depth signals |
