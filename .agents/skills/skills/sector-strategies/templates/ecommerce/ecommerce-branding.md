# E-commerce Branding & Visual Identity

E-commerce websites sell products in a split second. 75% of consumers judge credibility from visual design. The average shopper visits 3-4 sites before purchasing. Your visual identity determines whether they buy from you or your competitor.

## Color Psychology for E-commerce

| Hue | Psychological Effect | Best For | Avoid When |
|-----|---------------------|----------|------------|
| **Trust Blue** (#2563EB) | Reliability, security, calm | Electronics, SaaS, B2B products | Fashion, food, luxury |
| **Action Orange** (#EA580C) | Urgency, excitement, value | Flash sales, clearance, marketplace | Luxury, minimal, premium |
| **Premium Black** (#0F0F0F) | Luxury, sophistication, exclusivity | Fashion, jewellery, premium goods | Budget, bargain, family |
| **Fresh Green** (#16A34A) | Natural, healthy, sustainable | Organic food, eco products, wellness | Fast fashion, tech gadgets |
| **Passion Red** (#DC2626) | Urgency, energy, appetite | Food, clearance, impulse buys | Calm, wellness, premium |
| **Warm Gold** (#D97706) | Premium, quality, trust | Artisan goods, luxury food, crafts | Budget, discount, mass-market |
| **Coral Pink** (#F43F5E) | Feminine, trendy, youthful | Beauty, fashion, lifestyle | Professional, masculine, B2B |
| **Deep Purple** (#7C3AED) | Innovative, creative, premium | Tech products, beauty, digital goods | Traditional, conservative |

### Store Type Palettes

| Store Type | Primary | Accent | CTA | Reasoning |
|-----------|---------|--------|-----|-----------|
| **Luxury/Premium** | Black or Navy | Gold | White on dark | Exclusivity, sophistication |
| **Fashion** | Neutral + brand colour | Contrast accent | Bold, branded | Trend-forward, personality |
| **Food/Grocery** | Green or warm red | Fresh accent | Green "Add to Cart" | Natural, appetising |
| **Electronics/Tech** | Blue or dark gray | Electric accent | Blue "Buy Now" | Trust, innovation |
| **Handmade/Artisan** | Warm earth tones | Gold or copper | Warm CTA | Authenticity, craftsmanship |
| **Beauty/Cosmetics** | Blush or black | Rose gold accent | Soft CTA | Elegance, femininity |
| **Kids/Toys** | Bright, playful | Multiple accents | Fun, rounded CTA | Joyful, energetic |

## Typography for E-commerce

| Store Type | Header Font | Body Font | Key Rule |
|-----------|-------------|-----------|----------|
| **Luxury** | Elegant serif (Cormorant, DM Serif) | Clean sans | Generous spacing, premium feel |
| **Fashion** | Bold sans (Clash Display, Outfit) | Neutral sans (DM Sans) | Trend-forward, bold |
| **Food** | Warm serif or rounded sans (Nunito) | Readable sans | Appetising, friendly |
| **Tech** | Geometric sans (Space Grotesk, Rubik) | Clean sans (Inter) | Technical, precise |
| **Artisan** | Hand-drawn or distinctive serif (Fraunces) | Warm sans (Plus Jakarta Sans) | Personal, crafted |
| **Kids** | Rounded bold (Quicksand, Nunito) | Readable sans | Playful but clear |

## Photography Direction

- **Product shots**: Clean, white/neutral background, consistent lighting, multiple angles
- **Lifestyle shots**: Product in use, real context, aspirational but authentic
- **Detail shots**: Textures, materials, close-ups — build perceived quality
- **Scale reference**: Show size context (hand holding, next to common objects)
- **User-generated**: Real customer photos build trust more than studio shots
- **NEVER**: Misleading product photography — trust destruction is permanent

## Brand Voice

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Product descriptions** | Clear, benefit-focused | "Hand-stitched leather, built to last a lifetime" |
| **Urgency/sale** | Exciting but honest | "Flash sale: 30% off this weekend only" |
| **Reviews/social proof** | Authentic, real | "4.8/5 from 1,200+ happy customers" |
| **Trust/policy** | Reassuring, transparent | "Free returns within 30 days. No questions asked." |
