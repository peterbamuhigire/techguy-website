# Shopping Conversion & Trust Design

Average e-commerce conversion rate is 2-3%. Top stores achieve 5-8%. Cart abandonment averages 70%. The difference is design that removes friction, builds confidence, and makes checkout effortless.

## The 3-Second Test

A shopper landing on your store decides instantly:

- **"Do they sell what I need?"**: Clear product categories visible
- **"Is this legitimate?"**: Trust badges, reviews, professional design
- **"Can I afford it?"**: Price range indicator or featured products with prices
- **"Can I find it easily?"**: Search bar prominent, category navigation clear

## The Shopping Funnel

```
[Discovery]        →  [Browse]          →  [Evaluate]        →  [Purchase]
Homepage/landing      Category pages       Product detail       Cart → Checkout
Search/social         Filters/sort         Reviews/photos       Payment
Referral              Product grid         Compare options      Confirmation
```

### Page Flow
1. **Homepage**: Hero + featured → Categories → Best sellers → Reviews → Trust → Newsletter
2. **Category**: Filters (sidebar) → Product grid → Sort options → Pagination/infinite scroll
3. **Product**: Gallery → Price + CTA → Description → Reviews → Related products
4. **Cart**: Item list + quantities → Subtotal → Shipping estimate → Checkout CTA
5. **Checkout**: Guest option → Address → Shipping → Payment → Confirmation

## Product Page Design

The product page is where purchase decisions happen:

- **Gallery**: 4-6 images, zoom on hover, lifestyle + detail shots
- **Price**: Large, prominent, with comparison if on sale (~~$80~~ $59)
- **Add to Cart**: Large, primary colour, above fold, always visible
- **Trust badges**: Below CTA (secure checkout, free returns, shipping info)
- **Reviews**: Star rating aggregate + written reviews with photos
- **Description**: Benefits first, then specifications
- **Sizing/variants**: Clear selector with availability indication
- **Related products**: "You may also like" — 4 products max

## Trust Architecture

### Tier 1: Must-Have (Every Page)
- **Secure checkout badge**: SSL + payment logos (Visa, Mastercard, PayPal)
- **Return policy**: "Free 30-day returns" visible near CTA
- **Shipping info**: "Free shipping over $50" or clear shipping costs
- **Contact info**: Phone, email, chat visible in header/footer

### Tier 2: High-Impact (Product + Cart)
- **Customer reviews**: Star ratings + photos + verified badges
- **Stock indicators**: "In Stock", "Only 3 left" (honest)
- **Social proof**: "2,340 sold" or "Bestseller" badges
- **Product guarantees**: Warranty, authenticity, quality promise
- **Size guides**: Detailed with measurements (fashion)

### Tier 3: Depth (About + Policy Pages)
- **Company story**: Founders, mission, values
- **Sustainability**: Eco-friendly practices, packaging
- **Privacy policy**: Clear, accessible data handling
- **FAQ**: Shipping, returns, sizing, care instructions

## Cart & Checkout Optimisation

Cart abandonment is 70% — reduce friction:

- **Guest checkout**: Never force account creation (30% abandon if forced)
- **Progress indicator**: Step 1 of 3 (visible, encouraging)
- **Editable cart**: Change quantities/remove without leaving checkout
- **Shipping calculator**: Real-time shipping costs before final step
- **Multiple payment**: Credit card, PayPal, mobile money, bank transfer
- **Order summary**: Always visible during checkout
- **Trust badges**: Payment security logos near payment form
- **Confirmation**: Immediate email with order details + tracking promise

## Pricing Display

- **Clear pricing**: No hidden fees, no surprise costs at checkout
- **Sale pricing**: Show original + discounted with savings amount/percentage
- **Currency**: Local currency prominent, USD if international
- **Shipping**: Show in product card or early in flow — never surprise at checkout
- **Bundles**: "Save 15% when you buy 3" with visual comparison

## Common Mistakes
1. **Surprise shipping costs** — #1 reason for cart abandonment
2. **Forced account creation** — 30% will leave; offer guest checkout
3. **Low-quality product photos** — customers can't touch products online, photos must compensate
4. **No reviews** — 93% of shoppers read reviews before buying
5. **Complex checkout** — every extra field/step loses 10% of buyers
6. **Missing trust badges** — payment security logos reduce anxiety by 42%
7. **No mobile optimisation** — 60%+ e-commerce traffic is mobile
8. **Hidden return policy** — prominent return info increases conversion by 17%
