# Healthcare Facility Type Design Patterns

Each healthcare facility type serves different patients with different needs and anxieties.

## General Hospital

- **Palette:** Calm blue + white + soft green accent. Clean, institutional, reassuring.
- **Typography:** Accessible sans (Atkinson Hyperlegible or DM Sans). Maximum clarity.
- **Key elements:** Department directory, emergency info banner, doctor search, visitor information, campus map, insurance accepted
- **Unique:** Department finder, A&E wait times, visitor parking info, multilingual support

## Specialist Clinic

- **Palette:** Teal or deep blue + warm accent. Modern, expert, focused.
- **Typography:** Modern sans (Outfit, Plus Jakarta Sans). Professional, contemporary.
- **Key elements:** Condition-specific landing pages, specialist credentials, treatment options comparison, before/after (where appropriate), FAQ per condition
- **Unique:** Symptom checker, treatment comparison tool, specialist matching quiz

## Wellness & Whole-Person Care Centre

- **Palette:** Sage green + warm cream + blush accent. Natural, calming, whole-person.
- **Typography:** Soft serif or rounded sans (Nunito, Quicksand). Warm, approachable.
- **Key elements:** Service menu with descriptions, practitioner profiles, wellness blog, package pricing, booking calendar
- **Unique:** Wellness quiz ("What treatment is right for you?"), package comparison, practitioner booking

## Paediatric / Children's Health

- **Palette:** Warm blue + sunny yellow + soft coral. Friendly, safe, reassuring (for parents).
- **Typography:** Rounded sans (Nunito, Quicksand). Child-friendly but not childish.
- **Key elements:** Age-group service filtering, parent resources, immunisation schedules, growth tracking, child-friendly facility photos
- **Unique:** Age-based service finder, parent FAQ, child-friendly language toggles

## Mental Health & Counselling

- **Palette:** Lavender + soft blue + warm neutral. Calm, non-threatening, gentle.
- **Typography:** Calm sans (Jost, DM Sans). Never bold or aggressive. Light weights.
- **Key elements:** Confidentiality assurance, therapist matching, session types (online/in-person), crisis helpline visible, destigmatising language
- **Unique:** Therapist matching questionnaire, session type comparison, anonymous enquiry form, crisis resources prominent

## Dental Practice

- **Palette:** Clean teal + white + bright accent. Clean, modern, pain-free messaging.
- **Typography:** Modern clean sans (Plus Jakarta Sans, DM Sans). Fresh, clinical.
- **Key elements:** Treatment menu with pricing, before/after smile gallery, technology showcase, pain-free messaging, insurance info
- **Unique:** Smile assessment quiz, treatment cost estimator, before/after comparison module, fear-free messaging

## Diagnostic & Imaging Centre

- **Palette:** Deep blue or charcoal + clean white. Precise, technological, expert.
- **Typography:** Technical sans (Space Grotesk, Rubik). Precise, modern.
- **Key elements:** Test/scan menu, preparation instructions, results portal, referring doctor info, equipment list
- **Unique:** Test preparation checklist, online results access, referral form, scan type comparison

## Teaching / Research Hospital

- **Palette:** Deep navy + burgundy + gold accent. Academic authority, prestige, heritage.
- **Typography:** Traditional serif headers (DM Serif Display) + accessible sans body (DM Sans). Academic gravitas.
- **Key elements:** Department microsites, research publications, clinical trials recruitment, medical education programmes, residency/fellowship info, physician scientist profiles, grant/funding info
- **Unique:** Research publication feed, clinical trial finder, medical education portal, academic conference calendar, "Our Research in Numbers" stats section, dual audience (patients + medical professionals)

## Pharmacy / Dispensary

- **Palette:** Green + white + warm accent. Trust, health, accessibility.
- **Typography:** Clean, readable sans (DM Sans, Source Sans Pro). Clear, no-frills.
- **Key elements:** Medication search, prescription upload/transfer, delivery options, pharmacist profiles, operating hours, insurance accepted, health screening services
- **Unique:** Prescription refill/transfer form, medication reminder signup, pharmacist consultation booking, drug interaction checker link, vaccination services

## Branding Personality Framework

| Trait | Spectrum | Design Impact |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| **Warmth** | Clinical ←→ Nurturing | Colour temperature, photography intimacy, copy tone |
| **Technology** | Traditional ←→ High-tech | Equipment showcase, digital tools prominence |
| **Accessibility** | Standard ←→ Maximum | Font size, contrast, language simplicity |
| **Urgency** | Planned care ←→ Emergency | CTA prominence, contact info visibility |
| **Specialism** | Generalist ←→ Specialist | Service depth vs breadth, credential focus |
| **Scale** | Single practice ←→ Hospital network | Campus map, department navigation, multi-location |
