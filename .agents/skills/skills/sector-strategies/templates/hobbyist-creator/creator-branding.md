# Hobbyist/Creator Branding & Visual Identity

Creator websites sell personality, authenticity, and niche expertise. Visitors are choosing a PERSON to follow, support, or hire — not a faceless brand. The website must communicate: "This person is genuinely passionate, uniquely talented, and worth my time/money."

## Color Psychology for Creators

| Hue | Psychological Effect | Best For | Avoid When |
|-----|---------------------|----------|------------|
| **Electric Purple** (#7C3AED) | Creative, bold, unconventional | Digital artists, streamers, musicians | Traditional craft, photography |
| **Coral** (#F97316) | Warm, energetic, approachable | Food bloggers, lifestyle, fitness | Minimalist, moody photographers |
| **Teal** (#0D9488) | Fresh, modern, balanced | Tech YouTubers, educators, developers | Rustic crafters, vintage aesthetic |
| **Rose** (#E11D48) | Passionate, bold, expressive | Fashion, beauty, dance, performance | Woodworking, tech, finance |
| **Forest Green** (#166534) | Natural, grounded, sustainable | Gardeners, eco-crafters, outdoor creators | Electronic music, gaming |
| **Amber** (#D97706) | Warm, handmade, artisanal | Woodworkers, bakers, ceramicists | Tech reviewers, coders |
| **Charcoal** (#374151) | Sophisticated, minimal, serious | Photographers, writers, architects | Kids content, comedy, bright brands |
| **Indigo** (#4338CA) | Deep, thoughtful, premium | Educators, researchers, musicians | Casual lifestyle, food |

### Competitive Differentiation by Niche

| Creator Niche | Overused Colors | Differentiation Territory |
|---------------|----------------|--------------------------|
| **Tech/Dev** | Dark theme + neon green | Warm tones, light themes, unexpected accents |
| **Photography** | All black/white | One bold accent, warm gallery backgrounds |
| **Food/Cooking** | Red + warm tones | Cool accents (teal, sage), editorial feel |
| **Gaming/Streaming** | Neon purple + black | Pastel gaming, retro palettes, earth tones |
| **Craft/DIY** | Pinterest pastels | Bold, saturated, industrial, raw |
| **Fitness** | Black + red/orange | Sage green, lavender, sophisticated neutrals |
| **Writing/Blog** | Minimalist gray | Rich warmth, textured backgrounds, serif-heavy |

## Typography by Creator Type

| Creator Type | Header Font | Body Font | Personality |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Digital Artist** | Display/decorative (Clash Display) | Clean sans (DM Sans) | Bold, expressive |
| **Developer/Tech** | Geometric mono (Space Grotesk) | Clean sans (Inter, but customize) | Technical, precise |
| **Writer/Blogger** | Elegant serif (Cormorant) | Readable serif (Lora) | Literary, thoughtful |
| **Photographer** | Thin sans (Outfit 300) | Minimal sans (DM Sans) | Let images speak |
| **YouTuber/Streamer** | Bold sans (Plus Jakarta Sans 800) | Friendly sans (Nunito Sans) | Energetic, accessible |
| **Craftsperson** | Hand-drawn or slab serif (Zilla Slab) | Warm sans (Nunito) | Authentic, handmade |
| **Musician** | Display font (Cabinet Grotesk) | Clean sans (Satoshi) | Genre-dependent, distinctive |

### Font Rules for Creators

1. **Your font IS your brand** — more important here than any other sector
2. **Display fonts are encouraged** — unlike corporate, personality matters
3. **One rule: readability in body text** — headers can be wild, body must be legible
4. **Match your medium** — a calligrapher shouldn't use geometric sans

## Photography & Visual Direction

### Hero Image Strategy

| Creator Type | Hero Approach | Why |
|-------------|---------------|-----|
| **Visual creator** | Full-bleed best work | Let the work sell itself |
| **Personality-driven** | Professional photo of YOU | People follow people |
| **Product creator** | Hero product/creation | Show what they'll get |
| **Educator** | You teaching/presenting | Establish authority |

### Photo Guidelines

- **Authenticity over polish** — imperfect real beats perfect stock
- **Behind-the-scenes content** — workshops, studios, process shots build connection
- **Consistent filter/treatment** — creates recognisable visual brand
- **Show your face** — creators who show themselves get 38% more engagement
- **Work-in-progress** — people love seeing the process, not just results

## Brand Voice by Creator Personality

| Personality | Tone | Example CTA | Example Bio |
|------------|------|-------------|-------------|
| **Casual expert** | Friendly, knowledgeable | "Let's build something cool" | "I make things and teach you how" |
| **Thought leader** | Authoritative, generous | "Join 10K+ subscribers" | "Exploring the intersection of X and Y" |
| **Artisan** | Warm, meticulous | "See the collection" | "Handcrafted with care since 2018" |
| **Entertainer** | Playful, energetic | "Watch the latest" | "Making you laugh/think/create" |
| **Mentor** | Supportive, experienced | "Start your journey" | "Helping beginners become pros" |

### Voice Rules

1. **First person always** — "I" not "we" (unless it's a duo/team)
2. **Conversational, not corporate** — write like you talk
3. **Niche language welcome** — your audience knows the jargon
4. **Personality > professionalism** — be memorable, not vanilla
5. **Consistent across platforms** — website voice = social voice

## Logo & Personal Brand Mark

### Creator Logo Types

| Type | When to Use | Example |
|------|------------|---------|
| **Wordmark (your name)** | Writers, consultants, photographers | "Jane Smith" in distinctive font |
| **Monogram** | Artists, designers, minimalists | "JS" stylised |
| **Icon/mascot** | YouTubers, streamers, kid content | Illustrated character or symbol |
| **Signature** | Artists, calligraphers, luxury creators | Actual handwritten signature |
| **None** | Photographers, minimalists | Name in nav, no separate logo |

### Personal Brand Consistency

- Use the same profile photo across website + all social platforms
- Colour palette should match social media branding
- Website should feel like a "home base" that links out to platforms
