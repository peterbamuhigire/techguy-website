# Personal & Portfolio Branding & Visual Identity

Personal websites sell YOU before your work. Hiring managers spend 7 seconds scanning a portfolio. Freelance clients decide in the first scroll whether you "feel" like the right fit. Your visual identity IS your personal brand — it must be as distinctive as your work.

## Color Psychology for Portfolios

| Hue | Psychological Effect | Best For | Avoid When |
|-----|---------------------|----------|------------|
| **Charcoal/Black** (#1C1917) | Sophistication, power, editorial | Design, photography, fashion | You want approachable/friendly |
| **Deep Blue** (#1E40AF) | Trust, professionalism, reliability | Developer, engineer, consultant | Creative/artistic positioning |
| **Warm Orange** (#EA580C) | Energy, creativity, approachability | Illustrator, content creator, marketer | Corporate/formal positioning |
| **Forest Green** (#166534) | Growth, nature, calm expertise | Writer, sustainability, wellness | Fast-paced tech |
| **Purple** (#7C3AED) | Creativity, innovation, uniqueness | UX designer, artist, strategist | Conservative industries |
| **Coral** (#F43F5E) | Warmth, boldness, personality | Brand designer, social media, coach | Subtle/minimalist brands |
| **Teal** (#0D9488) | Modern, balanced, distinctive | Developer, product designer | Traditional/vintage aesthetic |
| **Warm Gray** (#78716C) | Minimal, elegant, lets work speak | Photographer, architect, fine artist | Bold personality-driven brands |

### Your Portfolio Should NOT Look Like Anyone Else's
- If every developer portfolio uses dark theme + green accent, choose light + warm accent
- If design portfolios in your niche use minimal black + white, go bold with colour
- Your colour palette should be as unique as your work

## Typography for Portfolios

| Creator Type | Header Font | Body Font | Personality |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Designer/Creative** | Display (Clash Display, Cabinet Grotesk) | Clean sans (DM Sans) | Bold, distinctive |
| **Developer/Engineer** | Monospace accent (JetBrains Mono, Fira Code) | Sans (Inter, Rubik) | Technical, precise |
| **Writer/Editor** | Elegant serif (Cormorant, Literata) | Readable serif (Lora, Source Serif) | Literary, thoughtful |
| **Photographer** | Minimal sans (Jost, Outfit) | Light sans (DM Sans) | Clean, lets images speak |
| **Illustrator/Artist** | Playful display (Fraunces, Quicksand) | Warm sans (Nunito) | Expressive, personal |
| **Consultant/Coach** | Modern sans (Plus Jakarta Sans, Satoshi) | Clean sans (Source Sans Pro) | Professional, approachable |

## Photography Direction

- **Professional headshot**: Authentic, well-lit, personality-showing (not corporate stiff)
- **Work environment**: Desk setup, studio, workspace — shows authenticity
- **Process shots**: Behind the scenes, sketches, work in progress
- **Event/speaking**: Conference photos, workshops, client interactions
- **Lifestyle**: Personality shots that reflect your brand (travel, hobbies relevant to niche)

## Brand Voice

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| **Hero/headline** | Confident, personal | "I design digital experiences that people love" |
| **About section** | Authentic, warm, professional | "10 years of turning complex problems into elegant solutions" |
| **Project descriptions** | Clear, outcome-focused | "Redesigned the checkout flow, increasing conversion by 34%" |
| **Blog/writing** | Knowledgeable, approachable | "Here's what I learned building 50+ client projects" |
| **Contact CTA** | Direct, inviting | "Have a project in mind? Let's talk." |
