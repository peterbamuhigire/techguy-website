# Professional Services Branding & Visual Identity

Professional services websites sell expertise and relationships. Clients are choosing a partner, not a product. The website must communicate competence, credibility, and chemistry — can they do the job, and would I want to work with them?

## Color Psychology for Professional Services

| Hue | Psychological Effect | Best For | Avoid When |
|-----|---------------------|----------|------------|
| **Deep Navy** (#1E3A5F) | Authority, trust, established | Accounting, legal, engineering | Creative agencies |
| **Charcoal** (#374151) | Sophistication, neutral, premium | Architecture, design, consulting | Community/warm services |
| **Forest Green** (#166534) | Growth, prosperity, reliability | Financial planning, agriculture | Fast-paced tech services |
| **Teal** (#0D9488) | Modern expertise, balanced | IT services, digital agencies | Traditional/heritage firms |
| **Burgundy** (#881337) | Prestige, heritage, strength | Law firms, established practices | Start-up agencies |
| **Warm Brown** (#78350F) | Earthy, trustworthy, established | Surveying, construction, real estate | Digital/tech services |
| **Slate Blue** (#475569) | Professional calm, reliability | HR services, administration | Bold creative services |

## Typography

| Service Type | Header Font | Body Font | Personality |
|-------------|-------------|-----------|-------------|
| **Accounting/Tax** | Classic serif (EB Garamond) | Clean sans (DM Sans) | Precise, trustworthy |
| **Legal** | Traditional serif (Cormorant) | Readable sans (Source Sans Pro) | Authoritative, formal |
| **Architecture/Design** | Modern sans (Outfit, Satoshi) | Clean sans (DM Sans) | Creative precision |
| **Engineering** | Geometric sans (Space Grotesk) | Technical sans (Rubik) | Technical, reliable |
| **IT/Digital** | Modern sans (Plus Jakarta Sans) | Clean sans (Inter) | Contemporary, expert |
| **Real Estate** | Elegant serif (DM Serif Display) | Clean sans (DM Sans) | Premium, trustworthy |
| **Marketing/PR** | Bold display (Clash Display) | Warm sans (Nunito Sans) | Creative, energetic |

## Brand Voice

| Audience | Tone | Example |
|----------|------|---------|
| **Prospective clients** | Confident, helpful | "Expertise that simplifies complexity" |
| **Existing clients** | Supportive, reliable | "Your success is our ongoing commitment" |
| **Talent/recruits** | Growth-oriented | "Build your career with industry leaders" |
