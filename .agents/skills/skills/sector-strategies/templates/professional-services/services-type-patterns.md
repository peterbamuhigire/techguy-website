# Services Type Design Patterns

Each professional service type has distinct client expectations and trust signals.

## Accounting & Tax

- **Palette:** Forest green + gold accent. Prosperity, precision, trust.
- **Typography:** Classic serif + clean sans. Formal, precise.
- **Key elements:** Service packages (tax, audit, advisory), deadline calendar, client portal, industry specialisations, compliance badges
- **Unique:** Tax deadline countdown, annual filing checklist, industry-specific service pages, fee structure transparency

## Legal Services

- **Palette:** Navy or burgundy + slate. Authority, heritage, gravitas.
- **Typography:** Traditional serif + readable sans. Formal, authoritative.
- **Key elements:** Practice areas, attorney profiles (bar admissions, jurisdictions), case results, FAQ by legal topic, consultation CTA
- **Unique:** Legal topic guides, jurisdiction map, attorney experience tracker, free initial consultation booking

## Architecture & Design

- **Palette:** Minimal. Black/charcoal + single vibrant accent. Let projects speak.
- **Typography:** Modern geometric sans. Clean, contemporary, design-conscious.
- **Key elements:** Project portfolio (large images), before/after, awards, process timeline, sustainability certifications
- **Unique:** Interactive project gallery, 3D walkthroughs, material palette showcase, award timeline

## Engineering & Construction

- **Palette:** Deep blue or warm brown + safety accent. Reliable, substantial, technical.
- **Typography:** Technical sans. Clear, structured, specification-friendly.
- **Key elements:** Project showcase with specs, capability statement, safety record, equipment list, certifications
- **Unique:** Project technical specifications, safety statistics, capability matrix, project timeline

## IT & Digital Services

- **Palette:** Teal or blue + electric accent. Modern, innovative, reliable.
- **Typography:** Modern geometric sans. Contemporary, tech-forward.
- **Key elements:** Service stack (cloud, security, support), technology partnerships, SLA guarantees, case studies with tech specs
- **Unique:** Technology stack visualiser, uptime dashboard, ROI calculator, tech partnership logos

## Real Estate

- **Palette:** Navy + gold or warm neutrals. Premium, trustworthy, aspirational.
- **Typography:** Elegant serif headers + clean sans. Premium, established.
- **Key elements:** Property listings with filters, virtual tours, agent profiles, neighbourhood guides, market reports
- **Unique:** Property search with map, mortgage calculator, virtual tour embeds, market data dashboard

## Marketing & PR

- **Palette:** Bold, creative. Brand personality-driven. Never generic corporate blue.
- **Typography:** Display fonts that show creative capability.
- **Key elements:** Portfolio/campaigns showcase, results metrics (ROI, reach), client logos, creative process
- **Unique:** Campaign results dashboard, creative brief generator, social proof metrics, press coverage gallery

## Branding Personality Framework

| Trait | Spectrum | Design Impact |
|-------|----------|---------------|
| **Formality** | Casual ←→ Formal | Typography, photography, voice tone |
| **Scale** | Solo/boutique ←→ Firm | Team prominence, office photos, client size |
| **Specialisation** | Generalist ←→ Niche | Service breadth, expertise depth |
| **Innovation** | Traditional ←→ Modern | Layout style, colour saturation, interactivity |
| **Price Position** | Accessible ←→ Premium | White space, imagery quality, CTA language |
