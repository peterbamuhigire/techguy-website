# Buyer Question Research Method

Use this method to turn raw buyer questions into strategic inputs for website planning.
This is not only a content-marketing exercise. It is a strategy and trust exercise.

## Core Principle

The questions buyers ask in sales calls, email threads, and proposals should shape:

- the strategy brief
- the trust architecture
- the page goal matrix
- the SEO topic and intent map
- future content assets

## Question Capture Sources

- sales calls
- discovery sessions
- proposal objections
- support conversations
- form submissions
- competitor comparison questions
- pricing questions
- internal team FAQs

## The Big 5 Capture

Always surface questions in these categories:

- price and cost
- problems and drawbacks
- comparisons and alternatives
- reviews
- benchmark / best-fit questions

## Strategy Uses

### For The Strategy Brief

Use buyer questions to define:

- objections
- decision criteria
- trust requirements
- CTA timing

### For SEO

Use buyer questions to build topic clusters around intent.

### For Copy

Use buyer questions to remove evasive language and increase specificity.

### For Sales

Use buyer questions to support assignment selling and proposal follow-up.

## Quality Rule

If the team keeps hearing a question and the site avoids it, the strategy is incomplete.
