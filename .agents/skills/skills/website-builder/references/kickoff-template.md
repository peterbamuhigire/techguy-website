## Kickoff Call Agenda

1. Reconfirm the business objective
2. Reconfirm the approved scope and exclusions
3. Confirm approvers and response windows
4. Confirm content responsibilities
5. Confirm technical access and dependencies
6. Confirm stage-gate workflow
7. Confirm first milestone and due dates

## Kickoff Rules

- If the sold scope and kickoff facts conflict, resolve the conflict immediately.
- If no approver is named, the project is not ready to start.
- If content ownership is unclear, record it as a delivery risk on day one.
- After kickoff, update the approvals log and stage-gate tracker the same day.
