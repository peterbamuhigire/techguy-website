## Company Name

Peter Bamuhigire — Technology & Business Consulting

## Tagline

Business management systems, developed, deployed, and maintained across Africa.

## What We Do

The practice is built around business management systems and custom software development, delivered as a single continuous discipline: develop, deploy, manage, maintain. This lifecycle commitment is the philosophical anchor — software is not handed over and forgotten, it is operated. Around this core, the practice builds websites for organisations whose digital presence carries commercial weight, and provides ICT strategy, business planning, and digital marketing as complementary advisory capabilities. With over 15 years of experience and a portfolio spanning more than 10 African countries, the practice has shipped flagship in-house platforms — Maduuka, Aqar, and Longhorn ERP — and continues to serve organisations across Africa and internationally that need a technology partner grounded in real African business environments.

## Who We Serve

Organisations that need custom business management systems built from scratch, ERP implementations rolled out across operations, or existing systems operated and maintained under clear service commitments. Organisations whose website is a commercial asset rather than a brochure — where the digital presence carries real weight in how the business is found, trusted, and transacted with. Specialised sectors served include property and real estate (through the practice's PropTech vertical), health products, agriculture, retail, restaurants, and pharmacies. And, more broadly, business owners and executives seeking ICT strategy, business planning, or digital marketing guidance from a partner with Pan-African reach and local market understanding.

## What Makes Us Different

Over 15 years of hands-on experience across more than 10 African countries — not theoretical knowledge, but practical solutions built in real business environments from Kampala to Dakar. Peter combines deep technical capability (full-stack development, ERP implementation, systems architecture) with genuine business consulting expertise (process analysis, strategic planning, M&E frameworks). This dual perspective — understanding both the technology and the business it serves — means solutions are built to deliver measurable results, not just technical milestones. Additionally, the practice has built its own flagship in-house systems — Maduuka (multi-tenant SaaS business management), Aqar (property management technology, operated directly by the practice), and Longhorn ERP (regional ERP delivery across East and West Africa) — demonstrating the ability to take solutions from concept to market across multiple countries.

Most distinctively, the practice commits to the full lifecycle. It does not only build software; it deploys it, operates it, and maintains it under SLA-backed retainers. Maduuka and Aqar are not historical references — they are systems the practice runs in production today. This is the strategic anchor: software as a continuous discipline, not a one-time deliverable.
