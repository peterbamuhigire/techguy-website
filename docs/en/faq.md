## Process & Engagement

### How does a typical consulting engagement work?

Every engagement follows a structured process: **Discovery** — an initial consultation to understand your organisation, challenges, and objectives. **Analysis** — a thorough assessment of your current systems, processes, and requirements. **Proposal** — a clear, detailed proposal outlining the recommended approach, timeline, deliverables, and investment. **Implementation** — hands-on delivery with regular progress updates. **Support** — ongoing assistance, training, and advisory to ensure your solutions continue to deliver value. The process is designed to ensure clarity and alignment at every stage.

## Products & Support

### What is Maduuka and who is it for?

Maduuka is a multi-tenant SaaS bookkeeping and business management platform designed specifically for SMEs. It includes modules for Point of Sale, Inventory Management, Sales, Accounting, Pharmacy, Restaurant, and Asset Management — all in one integrated system. The platform supports five languages (English, French, Arabic, Swahili, Spanish) and includes an Android mobile app with offline-first capability. Maduuka is ideal for business owners who need a reliable, affordable system to manage their day-to-day operations and gain clear visibility over their finances and stock.

### Do you work with international clients and remote teams?

Yes. Peter has extensive experience working with international clients and across time zones. His career includes three years of regional ICT management across West Africa, ongoing remote consulting for multinational organisations, and PropTech clients in the United Kingdom. Remote consultations, project management, and support are well-established parts of the practice. Support may include technical maintenance, user training, system updates, and strategic advisory depending on the nature of the engagement.
