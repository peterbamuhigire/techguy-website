# Services

The practice is built around one organising idea: business systems should be developed, deployed, managed, and maintained as a continuous discipline — not handed over and forgotten. The primary work is custom business management systems and software development for organisations that need their operations to run on technology built for them. Alongside this core practice, complementary website development and business advisory services are offered as separate, supporting capabilities for clients whose needs extend in those directions. Drawing on more than 15 years of work across over 10 African countries, most engagements begin as a project and become a long-term operational relationship.

## Business Management Systems & Software Development

**Short Description**: End-to-end business management systems and custom software — developed, deployed, managed, and maintained as a single continuous discipline.

**Detailed Description**: Business management systems are at the core of this practice. The work spans ERP implementations, multi-tenant SaaS platforms, custom internal systems, and Android mobile applications — all built to solve specific operational problems rather than to demonstrate technology. Every engagement follows the same lifecycle: develop, deploy, manage, and maintain. That commitment to the full lifecycle is what distinguishes the practice from one-shot project work, and it is demonstrated through in-house systems such as Maduuka (a multi-tenant SaaS business management platform) and Aqar (a property management platform), both of which are developed, operated, and maintained directly by the practice. Regional ERP delivery, including Longhorn ERP implementations, applies the same discipline to third-party systems.

### The Full Lifecycle

**Develop** — Requirements engineering, systems analysis, architecture, and build. Full-stack web applications in PHP, Python, and JavaScript; Android mobile apps in Kotlin with Jetpack Compose and offline-first design; multi-tenant SaaS platforms with clean API foundations. Every build is preceded by a thorough understanding of the business processes it must support.

**Deploy** — Environment setup, secure infrastructure provisioning, data migration from legacy systems and spreadsheets, user training, and phased rollout. Deployment is treated as its own discipline, not an afterthought, because most system failures originate at the handover.

**Manage** — Ongoing administration, user support, performance monitoring, access governance, and security oversight. Once a system is live, the practice can operate it on the client's behalf or work alongside an in-house team — whichever fits the organisation.

**Maintain** — Updates, feature evolution as the business changes, infrastructure care, and lifecycle planning. Maintenance retainers are SLA-backed and scoped to the criticality of the system, with clear response commitments rather than best-effort promises.

**Key Deliverables**:
- ERP system implementation and customisation (including regional Longhorn ERP delivery)
- Custom business management systems built to specific operational requirements
- SaaS platform architecture and multi-tenant system design
- Android mobile application development (Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, offline-first)
- Full-stack web application development (PHP, Python, JavaScript)
- Database design, optimisation, and management (MySQL, PostgreSQL)
- REST API design and third-party integration
- AI-assisted development workflows (Claude Code, LLM-powered tooling)
- Legacy system modernisation and structured data migration
- Ongoing system management and SLA-backed maintenance retainers

**Industries served**: health products distribution, agriculture, property management, retail, restaurants, pharmacies, and research institutions.

**Flagship in-house systems**: Maduuka (multi-tenant SaaS business management), Aqar (property management technology — the practice's PropTech vertical), and Longhorn ERP (regional ERP delivery across East and West Africa). These systems serve as proof that the develop-deploy-manage-maintain lifecycle is operated in practice, not merely described.

## How We Work

Every engagement follows the same five-stage process, from first conversation through long-term operation:

1. **Discovery** — An initial consultation to understand the organisation, its challenges, and its objectives. A conversation, not a sales pitch.
2. **Analysis** — A thorough assessment of current systems, processes, and requirements. Identifying what works, what does not, and where the opportunities lie.
3. **Proposal** — A clear, detailed proposal outlining the recommended approach, timeline, deliverables, and investment. No hidden costs, no vague promises.
4. **Implementation** — Hands-on delivery with regular progress updates. The practice works alongside the client's team, not in isolation.
5. **Support — Manage & Maintain** — For business management systems, software, and websites, the engagement does not end at handover. Ongoing administration, user support, performance monitoring, security oversight, feature evolution, and infrastructure care are offered under SLA-backed maintenance retainers. Most clients move into a long-term operational relationship at this stage, which is how the develop-deploy-manage-maintain lifecycle is delivered in practice.
