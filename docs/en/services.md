## How We Work

Every engagement follows a structured process designed to ensure clarity, alignment, and measurable results:

1. **Discovery** — An initial consultation to understand your organisation, challenges, and objectives. This is a conversation, not a sales pitch.
2. **Analysis** — A thorough assessment of your current systems, processes, and requirements. Identifying what works, what does not, and where the opportunities lie.
3. **Proposal** — A clear, detailed proposal outlining the recommended approach, timeline, deliverables, and investment. No hidden costs, no vague promises.
4. **Implementation** — Hands-on delivery with regular progress updates. Peter works alongside your team, not in isolation.
5. **Support** — Ongoing support, training, and advisory to ensure your solutions continue to deliver value long after the initial engagement.
