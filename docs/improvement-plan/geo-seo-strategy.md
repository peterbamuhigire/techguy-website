## Internal Linking Architecture

The geo pages must link to each other hierarchically:

```
Homepage (Uganda/Kampala — primary)
  ├── /en/it-consulting-uganda/ ──────► /en/it-consulting-east-africa/
  ├── /en/software-development-uganda/ ──► /en/software-development-east-africa/
  ├── /en/website-development-uganda/ ──► /en/website-development-east-africa/
  │
  ├── /en/it-consulting-east-africa/
  ├── /en/software-development-east-africa/
  ├── /en/website-development-east-africa/
  │
  └── Blog posts (support each geo page — 2-3 articles per page)

FR Homepage (Francophone Africa — primary FR)
  ├── /fr/consultant-informatique-afrique/
  ├── /fr/developpement-logiciel-afrique/
  └── /fr/developpement-web-afrique/
        └── FR Blog posts (2-3 articles per page)
```

**Rule:** Every Uganda page links to the corresponding East Africa page ("We also work across East Africa →"). Every East Africa page links back to the Uganda page as the home base. Every FR page links to the EN equivalent via hreflang AND a visible language-switch CTA.
