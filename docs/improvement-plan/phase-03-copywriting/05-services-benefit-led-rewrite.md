## Why This Matters

Buyers do not purchase features; they purchase outcomes. A business owner reading "Custom software solutions — from web applications and mobile apps to full SaaS platforms" is learning what Peter can do. A business owner reading "Software built so precisely for your business that your staff use it every day, without workarounds" is learning what will change for them. The second framing sells; the first informs.

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Service 1 — Software Development

**Features/Benefits Grid analysis:**

| Feature | Which means... | Benefit | So that... | Outcome |
|---------|---------------|---------|------------|---------|
| Custom-built software | the system is designed for your actual processes | No workarounds or manual data re-entry | your staff can do their core work | time saved, errors eliminated |
| Cross-border capability | systems work across your country network | No separate system per country | one view of the business regardless of geography | unified operations |
| Bilingual (EN/FR) | interface and support in both languages | Staff in Francophone and Anglophone offices use the same system | no language barrier to adoption | faster rollout, less training |

**New capability headline (current):** `"Software Development"` or `"Custom Software Solutions"`

**New benefit headline:**
```
Software built precisely for how your business actually operates — not adapted from something built for a different market
```

**New EN body copy:**
```
Off-the-shelf software is built for the average business. If your business operates across multiple African countries, manages inventory in French and English, or runs on workflows that do not match a standard ERP template — off-the-shelf software produces workarounds, not solutions.

Peter builds software that fits the specific reality of your organisation: your processes, your languages, your geographic spread, your staff's technical literacy. The result is a system that your people actually use — because it was built for the way they actually work.

Past engagements: ERP systems for pharmaceutical distribution across West Africa; property management platforms for Ugandan landlords; staff management systems for hospitality groups operating in multiple countries.
```

**New FR body copy:**
```
Les logiciels standard sont conçus pour l'entreprise moyenne. Si votre organisation opère dans plusieurs pays africains, gère des stocks en français et en anglais, ou fonctionne selon des processus qui ne correspondent pas à un modèle ERP standard — un logiciel standard produit des contournements, pas des solutions.

Peter conçoit des logiciels qui s'adaptent à la réalité spécifique de votre organisation : vos processus, vos langues, votre déploiement géographique, le niveau de compétence technique de votre personnel. Le résultat est un système que vos collaborateurs utilisent réellement — parce qu'il a été conçu pour leur façon de travailler.
```
