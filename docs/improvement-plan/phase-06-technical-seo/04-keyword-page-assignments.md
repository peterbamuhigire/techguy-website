## Title Tag Rules (apply to all pages)

1. **Length:** 50–65 characters optimal; maximum 90 characters.
2. **Format:** `[Primary keyword phrase] — [Benefit or context] | [Brand name]`
3. **Primary keyword:** appears at or near the start of the title.
4. **No keyword cannibalism:** each page targets a unique primary keyword — no two pages share the same primary keyword.
5. **No brand stuffing:** "Peter Bamuhigire" appears once, at the end, after a pipe separator.
6. **British spelling:** "optimisation" not "optimization", "organisation" not "organization".

## Meta Description Rules

1. **Length:** 150–160 characters optimal; maximum 180 characters.
2. **Include primary keyword naturally** (do not force it).
3. **Include one specific claim** (years, countries, languages, specific service).
4. **End with a soft CTA or benefit statement.**
5. **No duplicate descriptions** — every page has a unique description.
