## Target State

A fully set-up and verified Google Business Profile, with complete profile, consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone), 10 GBP post templates ready to publish, and a brief for obtaining the first five reviews.

## Why This Matters

The Google local pack (the 3-business map result that appears above organic search results) is displayed for searches like "IT consultant Kampala" and "software developer Uganda". A verified GBP with a complete profile and some reviews is the minimum requirement to appear in that pack. Without a GBP, the practice cannot appear in local pack results regardless of how strong the organic SEO is.

Reviews are the most important GBP ranking signal after profile completeness. The goal is 5 genuine reviews within 60 days of verification.

**Who to ask (in order of likelihood to respond):**

5. Any other client with whom there is an ongoing relationship

**How to ask:**
Send this message via WhatsApp (most effective in Uganda) or email:

Subject: A small request — a Google review

Dear [Name],

I hope you are well.

I am setting up the Google Business Profile for my practice and am hoping to gather some initial reviews from clients who have had direct experience of the work.

If you would be willing, a 2-3 sentence review on Google would be genuinely helpful — it does not need to be detailed, just honest. A link is below.

[Insert GBP review link — generated from Google Business Profile → "Ask for reviews" → copy link]

With thanks,
Peter

---

**Important:** Do not ask for reviews in exchange for anything, and do not script the review content. Google prohibits incentivised reviews and reviews that are not the reviewer's own words.
