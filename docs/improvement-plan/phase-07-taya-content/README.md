# Phase 7: TAYA Content — They Ask You Answer

## Score at Entry: 8.5/10 → Score at Exit: 8.7/10

Phase 6 made existing pages technically excellent. Phase 7 fills the content gaps that prevent the site from owning its search categories. Using the They Ask You Answer methodology — the Big 5 content pillars — this phase produces 15 new articles across three geographic clusters: Uganda, East Africa, and Francophone Africa (French).

The existing 12 articles are good but cover a mix of topics without a systematic strategy. Phase 7 takes a structured approach: every article targets a specific search intent, links to at least one geo landing page, and is written to the same standard as the rest of the site.

---

## The Big 5 Content Pillars

| Pillar | Topic type | Example for this practice |
|--------|-----------|--------------------------|
| 1 | Cost and pricing | "How much does software development cost in Uganda?" |
| 2 | Problems | "Why do ERP implementations fail in Africa?" |
| 3 | Comparisons | "Custom software vs. off-the-shelf for East African businesses" |
| 4 | Best of | "Best ICT consultants in Uganda: What to look for" |
| 5 | Reviews | "Is [product] right for Ugandan businesses?" |
