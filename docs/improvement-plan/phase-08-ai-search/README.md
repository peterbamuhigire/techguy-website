# Phase 8: AI and LLM Search Optimisation

## Score at Entry: 8.7/10 → Score at Exit: 8.9/10

Phase 7 produced the content. Phase 8 makes that content legible to AI systems — ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, Claude, and the AI overviews appearing in Google search results. These systems do not rank pages the way traditional search engines do. They synthesise answers from sources that demonstrate: clear structure, specific claims, verifiable credentials, and content that directly answers the questions being asked.

This phase builds four components: a QuickAnswer format for every article opener; FAQ blocks on all new articles; an author E-E-A-T schema that makes Peter's credentials machine-readable; GA4 custom channel groups to track AI referral traffic; and a citation transparency protocol that makes every factual claim in the site's content attributable.
