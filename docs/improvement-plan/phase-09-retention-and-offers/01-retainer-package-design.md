## Current State

No retainer or ongoing support packages exist on the site or in the practice. Engagements end with project completion. The only commercial relationship available to clients after a project is to commission a new project.

From the analysis: "Client retention score: 3.0/10. No retainer model. No recurring revenue. No defined post-project relationship. Every client engagement ends with a completed deliverable and no structured path to continued engagement."

## Target State

Five named retainer packages, each with a clear scope, monthly commitment, and pricing guidance. These packages are referenced on the services page, the new pricing page (Phase 9, file 04), and in client communications after project completion.

## Package Designs

Each package follows the format: name → who it is for → what is included → what is not included → pricing guidance → CTA.

---

### Package 1 — Technology Care

**Who it is for:** Organisations with a system Peter built or implemented that need ongoing maintenance, updates, and first-line support.

**What is included:**
- Software updates: security patches, bug fixes, minor UI improvements (up to 4 hours per month)
- Uptime monitoring and basic server health check
- Monthly 30-minute status call
- Priority response to support requests (within 4 business hours vs. 1-2 business days for non-retainer clients)
- Annual system health report

**What is not included:**
- Major new features or modules (scoped and priced separately)
- Infrastructure changes (scoped separately)
- Staff training (available as add-on)

**Pricing guidance:** UGX 600,000–1,200,000 per month (USD 165–330) depending on system complexity. A specific monthly fee is agreed before the retainer begins.

**Who it is not for:** Organisations that need strategic ICT leadership — see the ICT Manager package.

**Full copy (for use on pricing page and in client communications):**
```
Technology Care — ongoing maintenance for systems this practice has built or implemented.

Most technology systems degrade without maintenance — not because they are poorly built, but because the environment they operate in changes: security vulnerabilities are discovered, dependencies are updated, and small issues accumulate. Technology Care addresses this before problems become crises.

You receive: monthly software updates, uptime monitoring, a 30-minute monthly status call, priority response to support requests, and an annual system health report. Everything needed to keep your system running reliably.

Starting from UGX 600,000 per month. Specific fee agreed before the retainer begins.
```

### Package 2 — SEO Foundation

**Who it is for:** Organisations with a website built by this practice that want to appear in Google search results for their primary keywords within 12 months.

**What is included:**
- Monthly keyword ranking report (primary keywords tracked)
- Monthly on-page SEO update (1-2 pages optimised per month)
- Google Search Console monitoring and issue resolution
- Google Business Profile post publishing (1 post per week — 4 per month)
- Quarterly content brief (one article topic per quarter, brief provided — writing is client's responsibility or available as add-on)
- Monthly 30-minute SEO review call

**What is not included:**
- Article writing (available as add-on at per-article rate)
- Link building (not offered — focus is on on-page SEO and content)
- Paid advertising management

**Pricing guidance:** UGX 500,000–800,000 per month (USD 140–220).

**Full copy:**
```
SEO Foundation — monthly SEO maintenance for websites built by this practice.

A website without ongoing SEO maintenance will rank for the keywords it launches on and gradually lose ground to competitors who update their content and optimise their pages regularly. SEO Foundation prevents that.

You receive: monthly keyword ranking report, monthly on-page page optimisation, Search Console monitoring, weekly Google Business Profile posts, and a quarterly content brief. Structured, consistent SEO work — not a one-time fix.

Starting from UGX 500,000 per month.
```

### Package 3 — Content Growth

**Who it is for:** Organisations that want to build organic traffic and thought leadership through a regular publication schedule, without committing the internal staff time to write articles.

**What is included:**
- Two blog articles per month (1,200–1,800 words each), written by this practice
- Articles optimised for primary keywords agreed at the start of the retainer
- FAQPage schema and QuickAnswer added to each article
- Published and live on the client's website
- Monthly performance report (views, time on page, leads from content)

**What is not included:**
- Social media promotion of articles (available as add-on)
- Video or podcast production

**Pricing guidance:** UGX 1,500,000–2,500,000 per month (USD 410–685). Price varies based on article complexity and research requirements.

**Full copy:**
```
Content Growth — two articles per month, written, optimised, and published by this practice.

Content marketing works — but it requires consistent publication over months, not occasional articles. Content Growth provides the consistency without requiring a full-time writer on the client's team.

Two articles per month, written by an ICT professional who understands the market, optimised for search, and published directly to the client's website. The result is a growing library of credibility-building content that attracts organic traffic and demonstrates expertise.

Starting from UGX 1,500,000 per month.
```

### Package 4 — CRO Monitoring

**Who it is for:** Organisations with an established website that want to systematically improve the proportion of visitors who take the desired action (fill in a form, make a call, request a quote).

**What is included:**
- Monthly CRO audit (one section of the site reviewed per month)
- One implementation change per month (copy rewrite, CTA change, form simplification)
- Heatmap or session recording review (requires client to install a tool — Hotjar free tier is sufficient)
- A/B test design (one test per quarter)
- Monthly performance report (conversion rate, form submissions, call clicks)

**What is not included:**
- Full website redesign
- Major new features

**Pricing guidance:** UGX 700,000–1,000,000 per month (USD 190–275).

**Full copy:**
```
CRO Monitoring — systematic conversion rate improvement for established websites.

Most websites are built and launched — and then left. Visitor behaviour changes, competitive context changes, and the copy that converted visitors in the first year becomes stale in the second. CRO Monitoring prevents the gradual decline of an established website's conversion rate.

Monthly audits, monthly implementation changes, quarterly A/B tests, and a monthly performance report. Consistent, evidence-based improvement — not guesswork.

Starting from UGX 700,000 per month.
```

### Package 5 — ICT Manager (Fractional)

**Who it is for:** Organisations that need ongoing ICT leadership — strategy, vendor management, staff training, systems oversight — but do not need or cannot afford a full-time internal ICT Manager.

**What is included:**
- 8-16 hours per month of dedicated ICT management time (on-site or remote)
- Attendance at relevant management or board meetings (on request)
- ICT vendor contract review and negotiation support
- ICT incident response coordination
- Quarterly ICT strategy review
- Staff ICT training (one session per quarter)
- Priority response for all ICT issues

**What is not included:**
- Software development (scoped separately)
- Hardware procurement (advised on, not purchased)
- 24/7 on-call coverage (standard hours 08:00-17:00 EAT)

**Pricing guidance:** UGX 2,500,000–5,000,000 per month (USD 685–1,370) depending on hours and complexity.

**Full copy:**
```
ICT Manager (Fractional) — ongoing ICT leadership for organisations that need strategy without a full-time hire.

A full-time ICT Manager in Uganda costs between UGX 3,000,000 and UGX 7,000,000 per month. Many organisations need the leadership, not the full-time position. Fractional ICT Manager provides 8-16 hours per month of dedicated ICT management — strategy, vendor oversight, staff training, incident response, and board-level reporting — at a fraction of the full-time cost.

This arrangement works best for organisations with 20-150 staff, a meaningful technology footprint, and ICT decisions that are currently being made reactively rather than strategically.

Starting from UGX 2,500,000 per month.
```

## Retainer Onboarding Protocol

Every retainer begins with an onboarding session before billing starts:

1. **Technology Care:** 2-hour system documentation session — access credentials, architecture map, known issues logged
2. **SEO Foundation:** Keyword audit and GSC verification — current rankings recorded as baseline
3. **Content Growth:** Editorial planning session — 3-month article calendar agreed, primary keywords confirmed
4. **CRO Monitoring:** Baseline audit session — current conversion rate recorded, priority pages identified
5. **ICT Manager:** Full ICT audit — infrastructure map, vendor contracts reviewed, risk assessment produced

The onboarding session is billed at the monthly rate (not an additional charge) — it is the first month's work.
