# Phase 10: Elite — The Final 1%

## Score at Entry: 9.1/10 → Score at Exit: 9.5/10

The previous nine phases built a world-class professional services website. Phase 10 addresses the elements that separate a very good site from an exceptional one: a founder video that transmits trust at a level no written text can match; a proof library that gives the practice a systematic way to collect and deploy social proof; advanced structured data formats; a social media amplification strategy that extends the site's reach; and a measurement framework that makes every strategic decision evidence-based.

Not all Phase 10 elements are immediately achievable — the founder video requires filming; the social proof library requires client relationships. Phase 10 is the ongoing practice, not a sprint. The documents in this phase are as much operational guides as implementation briefs.
